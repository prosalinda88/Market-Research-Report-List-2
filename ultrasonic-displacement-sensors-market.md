<p><p>Ultrasonic displacement sensors are categorized into two types: analog type and digital type. Analog type sensors convert the distance measurement into an analog signal output, which can be further processed by a control system. On the other hand, digital type sensors provide a direct digital measurement of the distance. They utilize advanced microprocessors to convert the ultrasonic signals into digital data, making it easier to integrate with other digital systems. Both types of sensors are widely used in various industries for precise and non-contact distance measurements.</p></p>
