<p><p>The large-scale reed switch market primarily consists of four major types: Form A, Form B, Form C, and Others. Form A reed switches are normally open contacts that are closed when a magnetic field is applied, while Form B switches are normally closed contacts that open when a magnetic field is present. Form C switches have a common terminal and two switches, allowing for both normally open and normally closed contacts. The "Others" category encompasses any other specialized variations or combinations of reed switches to meet specific industry requirements.</p></p>
