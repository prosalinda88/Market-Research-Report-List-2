<p><p>Service Desk Tools are software applications used by IT service providers to manage and address customer inquiries, issues, and service requests. The market for these tools can be classified into two main types: Cloud-based and On-premise. </p><p>Cloud-based service desk tools are hosted on the internet and accessible through a web browser. They offer flexibility, scalability, and low upfront costs as they are managed by third-party providers. On the other hand, On-premise tools are installed locally on the organization's own servers and managed by their IT teams. They offer more control and customization options but require higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance.</p></p>
