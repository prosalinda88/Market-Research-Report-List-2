<p><p>The triangular bandage market consists of two main types: sterile triangular bandages and non-sterile triangular bandages. Sterile triangular bandages are individually packaged and are primarily used in medical settings where cleanliness and infection prevention are crucial. They are commonly used to support and immobilize injured limbs and can also be used as a dressing. Non-sterile triangular bandages, on the other hand, are not individually packaged and are often used in first aid kits and emergency situations where immediate treatment is needed. They serve similar purposes as sterile triangular bandages but may not offer the same level of sterility.</p></p>
