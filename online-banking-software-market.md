<p><p>The Online Banking Software Market can be divided into two types - Cloud-based and On-premises. Cloud-based online banking software refers to a system where the software and data are stored on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It offers convenience, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, On-premises online banking software is installed and operated on the user's own servers and infrastructure. It gives users control over their data and software but may require higher initial investment and maintenance costs.</p></p>
