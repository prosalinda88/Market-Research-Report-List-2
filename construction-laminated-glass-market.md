<p><p>Construction laminated glass is a type of safety glass that is widely used in buildings and other structures due to its strength and durability. It is made by sandwiching a layer of transparent polymer, usually polyvinyl butyral (PVB), between two or more layers of glass. This process is carried out under high heat and pressure, which bonds the layers together and creates a strong composite material.</p><p>The construction laminated glass market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for safety and security in buildings, along with the stringent building codes and regulations, is driving the market growth.
