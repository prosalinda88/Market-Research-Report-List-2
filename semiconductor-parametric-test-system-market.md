<p><p>The Semiconductor Parametric Test System market includes two main types of testing: Static Parameter Testing and Dynamic Parameter Testing. Static Parameter Testing evaluates the semiconductor's performance under stable conditions, measuring characteristics like voltage and leakage current. Dynamic Parameter Testing, on the other hand, assesses the semiconductor's behavior in changing or variable conditions, focusing on aspects such as frequency response and power consumption. Both types of testing play crucial roles in ensuring the quality and reliability of semiconductor devices, contributing to their overall market value and customer satisfaction.</p></p>
