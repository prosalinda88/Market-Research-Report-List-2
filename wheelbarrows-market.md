<p><p>The wheelbarrows market encompasses two main types: traditional wheelbarrows and hand trucks. Traditional wheelbarrows are simple, single-wheeled carts used for carrying and transporting various materials, typically with a capacity of one to two cubic yards. They are commonly used in construction, gardening, and other manual labor-intensive tasks. On the other hand, hand trucks, also known as dolly carts, are two-wheeled upright carts designed for moving heavier and bulkier goods. They often have a folding feature for easy storage and are widely utilized in warehouses, delivery services, and moving companies. Both types play crucial roles in facilitating manual transportation needs across various industries.</p></p>
