<p><strong>The Reflection Telescope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Main Focus System</li><li>Newton System</li><li>Cassegrain System</li><li>Gregorian System</li><li>Folding Shaft System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reflection telescope market offers various types of telescopes with different focus systems. The main focus system is a simple design where light enters the tube and reflects off a curved primary mirror before passing through a secondary mirror. Newton system telescopes have a small diagonal mirror directing light from the primary mirror to the eyepiece. Cassegrain system telescopes use a larger secondary mirror to reflect light back through a hole in the primary mirror. Gregorian system telescopes have a concave secondary mirror to reflect light back through a hole in the primary mirror. Folding shaft system telescopes have a folding design with multiple mirrors to redirect light. Lastly, there are other market variations available as well.</p></p>
