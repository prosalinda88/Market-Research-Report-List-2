<p><p>The lithium battery manufacturing equipment market consists of three main types: pretreatment, cell assembly, and post-processing. Pretreatment involves preparing the raw materials, such as cathode and anode, for cell assembly. Cell assembly refers to the process of integrating these materials into battery cells. Post-processing involves the final stages of battery production, such as the addition of electrolytes and encasing the cells. Each type plays a critical role in lithium battery manufacturing, ensuring the efficiency and reliability of the final product.</p></p>
