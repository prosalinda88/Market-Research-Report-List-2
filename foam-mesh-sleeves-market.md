<p><p>Foam mesh sleeves are used for protective packaging and cushioning applications. The market for foam mesh sleeves can be classified into three types: Expanded Polyethylene foam (EPE), Polyethylene (PE), and Polypropylene (PP). EPE foam mesh sleeves are made from expanded polyethylene, which is a lightweight and flexible material known for excellent shock absorption. PE foam mesh sleeves are made from polyethylene and offer good impact resistance and durability. PP foam mesh sleeves are made from polypropylene, which provides high tensile strength and resistance to chemicals and moisture.</p></p>
