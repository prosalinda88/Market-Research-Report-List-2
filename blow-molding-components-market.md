<p><p>Blow molding is a manufacturing process used to produce various hollow plastic components. There are three main types of blow molding: extrusion, injection, and stretch. </p><p>Extrusion blow molding involves melting plastic resin and pushing it through a die to produce continuous tubes, which are then inflated to create the desired shape.</p><p>Injection blow molding combines elements from both injection molding and blow molding. It starts with an injection molded preform, which is then transferred to a blow molding cavity and inflated.</p><p>Stretch blow molding is commonly used for producing bottles. It entails stretching a preform to its desired shape using both axial and circumferential directions, followed by inflation to achieve the final product.</p></p>
