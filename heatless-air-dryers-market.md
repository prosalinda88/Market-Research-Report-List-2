<p><p>Heatless air dryers are devices used to remove moisture from compressed air without using heat. They work on the principle of adsorption, where the moisture present in the air is adsorbed onto a desiccant material such as activated alumina or silica gel. These dryers are commonly used in applications where heat-sensitive materials or processes are involved, as they eliminate the risk of heat damage.</p><p>The future outlook for the heatless air dryers market is positive, with a projected growth rate of 14.2% during the forecasted period.
