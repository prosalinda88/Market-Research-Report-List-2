<p><p>The FCC Catalyst Additive market consists of various types of additives used in fluid catalytic cracking processes. Octane Number Improving Agents are additives that enhance the octane rating of gasoline. Light Olefins Enhancing Agents are used to increase the production of light olefins such as propylene and butylene. Sulphur Reducing Agents minimize the sulfur content in fuel. Metal Passivation Agents decrease the harmful effects of metals in the catalyst. Additionally, there are various other additives that perform functions such as enhancing thermal stability and reducing coke formation in the FCC process.</p></p>
