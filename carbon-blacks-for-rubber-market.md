<p><p>Carbon blacks are types of fillers used in the rubber industry to enhance the performance and properties of rubber materials. Thermal black is a type of carbon black produced by the decomposition of natural gas at high temperatures. It provides excellent reinforcement, high abrasion resistance, and good processability. Furnace black is another type produced by the incomplete combustion of hydrocarbons. It offers high reinforcement, low rolling resistance, and good tear strength. Both types have different properties and are used based on the specific application requirements in the rubber industry.</p></p>
