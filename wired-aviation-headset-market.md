<p><strong>The Wired Aviation Headset Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Noise Reduction (PNR) Headsets</li><li>Active Noise Reduction (ANR) Headsets</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Wired Aviation Headset Market consists of two main types: Passive Noise Reduction (PNR) Headsets and Active Noise Reduction (ANR) Headsets. PNR Headsets rely on sound-absorbing materials and physical insulation to reduce noise, providing basic noise reduction capabilities. On the other hand, ANR Headsets use advanced technology, such as built-in microphones and electronic circuitry, to actively cancel out or reduce unwanted noise, offering superior noise reduction performance. Both PNR and ANR Headsets cater to the needs of aviation professionals and enthusiasts, but ANR Headsets generally offer enhanced noise reduction features.</p></p>
