<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Industrial Fire Sprinklers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Sprinkler</li><li>Wet Sprinkler</li><li>Deluge Sprinkler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial fire sprinklers market comprises three types: dry sprinklers, wet sprinklers, and deluge sprinklers. Dry sprinklers are specifically designed for environments with low temperatures and are filled with compressed air or nitrogen, releasing water only when a fire is detected. Wet sprinklers are the most common type, constantly filled with water and activated individually when a fire occurs. On the other hand, deluge sprinklers are open and provide a continuous flow of water when triggered, ideal for areas with highly flammable materials or rapid fire spread.</p></p>
