<p><p>Pulmonary needles, also known as lung biopsy needles, are medical devices used for collecting tissue samples from the lungs for diagnostic purposes. These needles are inserted into the lungs to extract tissue samples from abnormal lung nodules, masses, or lesions, which are then analyzed to diagnose lung diseases such as lung cancer, tuberculosis, or pulmonary fibrosis.</p><p>The market growth analysis of the pulmonary needles market indicates a positive outlook. The increasing prevalence of lung diseases, particularly lung cancer, is one of the key factors driving the market growth. According to the World Health Organization (WHO), lung cancer is the leading cause of cancer-related deaths globally. Moreover, advancements in technology, such as the development of minimally invasive techniques for lung biopsy, are fueling the demand for pulmonary needles.</p><p>Another factor contributing to market growth is the rising geriatric population worldwide. As age is a significant risk factor for lung diseases, the increasing elderly population is expected to drive the demand for pulmonary needles in the coming years.</p><p>The market is also witnessing several trends. There is a growing preference for robotic-assisted lung biopsies, which provide improved accuracy and allow for better visualization of the targeted lesion. Additionally, the shift towards image-guided biopsy procedures, such as computed tomography (CT)-guided lung biopsies, is gaining traction due to their higher success rates and reduced complications.</p><p>Overall, the pulmonary needles market is poised for substantial growth.
