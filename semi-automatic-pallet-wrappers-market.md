<p><p>Semi-automatic pallet wrappers are machines used in the packaging industry to wrap palletized goods. There are two main types of semi-automatic pallet wrappers: low profile and high profile. Low profile machines are compact, designed for small spaces, and have a low base height. They are ideal for lower volume operations. On the other hand, high profile machines are larger, provide higher throughput, and can handle heavier loads. They are suitable for high volume operations with larger packaging requirements. Both types of pallet wrappers offer efficiency and convenience in pallet wrapping processes.</p></p>
