<p><p>Audio drama refers to a form of storytelling that is presented in an audio format, utilizing audio techniques such as narration, dialogue, sound effects, and music to create a rich, immersive experience for the listeners. With the absence of visual elements, audio dramas rely solely on the power of sound to engage and captivate the audience. They often cover a wide range of genres, including fiction, mystery, science fiction, fantasy, horror, and comedy.</p><p>The audio drama market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue expanding at a rapid pace.
