<p><p>Dispensing metallic nozzles are used in various industries for precise and controlled liquid or gas dispensing applications. The market offers three main types of these nozzles: single, twin, and triple nozzle. Single nozzles are characterized by a single opening, allowing the dispensing of one substance at a time. Twin nozzles have two outlets, facilitating the simultaneous dispensing of two different substances. Triple nozzles have three outlets, enabling the concurrent dispensing of three separate substances. These different types cater to the specific needs of industries requiring varying levels of dispensing complexity and efficiency.</p></p>
