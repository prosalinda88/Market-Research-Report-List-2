<p><p>The electromagnetic switch market includes various types such as attracted armature, induction disc, induction cup, balanced beam, moving coil, and polarized moving iron switches. Attracted armature switches are operated by an armature attracted to a magnetic field, while induction disc switches rely on electromagnetic induction for actuation. Induction cup switches consist of a cup-shaped actuator, balanced beam switches use a balanced beam mechanism, moving coil switches have a coil that moves within a magnetic field, and polarized moving iron switches use a polarized iron core for actuation.</p></p>
