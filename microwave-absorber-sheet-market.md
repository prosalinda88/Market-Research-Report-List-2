<p><p>Microwave absorber sheet market types can be categorized based on their permeability levels. Low permeability microwave absorber sheets are designed to restrict the passage of microwaves, minimizing their ability to penetrate through the material. These sheets are suitable for applications where minimal electromagnetic interference is desired. On the other hand, high permeability microwave absorber sheets allow for the relatively easy passage of microwaves through the material. These sheets are used in applications where a higher level of absorption is required to dissipate electromagnetic waves effectively.</p></p>
