<p><p>Portable air purifiers are designed to improve indoor air quality by removing harmful pollutants. There are several types of air purifiers available in the market. HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) filters are highly effective in capturing particles like dust, pollen, and pet dander. Activated carbon filters can absorb gases and odors. Static electricity purifiers use an electric charge to capture particles. Ion and ozone generators release ions or ozone to neutralize pollutants. Other types may include UV-C filters and photoelectrochemical oxidation filters. Each type has its own unique mechanism for purifying the air and addressing specific air quality concerns.</p></p>
