<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Fibre laser machines are classified into two market types: pulsed fibre laser and continuous wave (CW) fibre laser. Pulsed fibre lasers produce high-intensity laser pulses for short durations, making them suitable for applications that require high peak power, such as laser marking and engraving. On the other hand, CW fibre lasers emit a continuous stream of laser beams, ensuring stable and constant power output. They are commonly used in industries like manufacturing and material processing, where precision and consistent power are crucial for cutting, welding, and drilling operations.</p></p>
