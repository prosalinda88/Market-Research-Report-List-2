<p><p>Sign lighting market types refer to different styles or designs of shades used to illuminate signs. Angle shades are light shades with an angled shape that offer directional lighting. Cone shades feature a conical shape, casting light downwards in a focused beam. Dome shades have a rounded shape, diffusing light in various directions. Eliptical shades are oval-shaped, providing a wide spread of light. Emblem shades have patterns or logos on them for decorative purposes. Other market types may include unique or custom-designed shades that do not fall under the aforementioned categories.</p></p>
