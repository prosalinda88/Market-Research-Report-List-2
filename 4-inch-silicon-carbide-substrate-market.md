<p><p>The 4 Inch Silicon Carbide Substrate Market is categorized into two types: Conductivity and Semi-Insulated. Conductivity refers to substrates that have high electrical conductivity, allowing the flow of electric current. These substrates are commonly used in electronic devices such as power electronics, high-power radio frequency (RF) devices, and light-emitting diodes (LEDs). On the other hand, Semi-Insulated substrates have low electrical conductivity, impeding the flow of electric current. They are primarily utilized in applications that require excellent thermal conductivity, such as high-temperature electronics, power modules, and semiconductor processing equipment.</p></p>
