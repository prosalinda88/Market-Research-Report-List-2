<p><p>An industrial electric generator is a device that converts mechanical energy into electrical energy for use in various industrial applications. Industrial electric generators are usually powered by diesel, natural gas, or propane engines.</p><p>The industrial electric generator market has been experiencing steady growth in recent years.
