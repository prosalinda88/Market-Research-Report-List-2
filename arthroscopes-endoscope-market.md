<p><p>The arthroscopes endoscope market includes three main types: straight, semi-flexible, and bent. Straight arthroscopes are rigid and allow for straightforward viewing and manipulation within the joints. Semi-flexible arthroscopes offer increased flexibility while maintaining some degree of rigidity, allowing for better navigation in complex joint structures. Bent arthroscopes are highly flexible and can reach difficult areas within joints with ease, offering improved visibility and access. These different market types cater to varying needs and preferences of medical professionals performing arthroscopic procedures.</p></p>
