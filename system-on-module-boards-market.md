<p><p>System on Module (SoM) boards are compact computing solutions that integrate a microprocessor with other essential components on a single module. These modules are available in various market types, including ARM, X86, Power PC, and Others. ARM-based SoMs utilize low-power consumption processors suitable for mobile devices and IoT applications. X86-based SoMs are powered by Intel or AMD processors, commonly found in PCs and servers. Power PC SoMs utilize IBM's Power Architecture, commonly used in networking and embedded systems. Lastly, Other types refer to a diverse range of processors like MIPS, RISC-V, and others, catering to specific applications and niche markets.</p></p>
