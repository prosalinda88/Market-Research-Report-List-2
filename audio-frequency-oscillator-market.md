<p><strong>The Audio Frequency Oscillator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>R-C Oscillator</li><li>L-C Oscillator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audio frequency oscillator market consists of two main types: R-C and L-C oscillators. R-C oscillators utilize a resistor and a capacitor to generate an oscillating signal at a desired audio frequency. These oscillators are simple and cost-effective, making them suitable for low-frequency applications and consumer devices. On the other hand, L-C oscillators employ an inductor and a capacitor to generate the desired audio frequency. They offer high stability and accuracy, making them more suitable for precise audio applications such as professional audio equipment and instrumentation.</p></p>
