<p><p>Office dividers are essential elements in creating privacy and dividing space in an office environment. The market offers two main types of dividers: desk-mounted and floor-standing. Desk-mounted dividers are typically attached to the edge of a desk, providing a partition between workstations. They are compact and easily adjustable. On the other hand, floor-standing dividers are larger and freestanding structures that can be placed anywhere in the office. They provide a more substantial division of space, allowing for greater privacy and separation between areas. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the office design.</p></p>
