<p><p>The Jet Hand Dryer market can be categorized into two types: Hand-in and Hand-under. Hand-in dryers are designed with a narrow opening where users insert their hands entirely into the device to dry them. Hand-under dryers, on the other hand, have a smaller opening and users place their hands beneath the device, allowing the air to dry them. Both types function similarly by emitting high-speed jets of air, but their design and usage differ slightly.</p></p>
