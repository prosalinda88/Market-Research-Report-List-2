<p><strong>The Ho Doped Fibers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Clad Fiber</li><li>Double Clad Fiber</li><li>Triple Clad Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Doped fibers are classified into three types based on the arrangement of their inner cladding layers. Single clad fibers have a single layer of cladding, which surrounds the doped core. Double clad fibers have two layers of cladding, with the inner cladding surrounding the doped core, and the outer cladding surrounding the inner cladding. Triple clad fibers have three layers of cladding, with the innermost cladding surrounding the doped core, the middle cladding surrounding the inner cladding, and the outer cladding surrounding the middle cladding. These different cladding arrangements enable various applications in optical communications and laser systems.</p></p>
