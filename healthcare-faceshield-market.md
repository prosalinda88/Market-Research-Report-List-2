<p><p>The healthcare faceshield market can be categorized into two main types - disposable and reusable. </p><p>Disposable faceshields are designed for single-use and are made of lightweight materials such as plastic. They provide a cost-effective solution, as they can be easily discarded after each use, reducing the risk of contamination. </p><p>On the other hand, reusable faceshields are made of more durable materials, such as polycarbonate. These shields can be cleaned and disinfected for multiple uses, making them a more sustainable option. They are typically used in settings where there is a lower risk of contamination and require regular maintenance to ensure proper hygiene standards.</p></p>
