<p><p>Crankcase heaters are devices used in heating systems to prevent the condensation of refrigerant or oil inside the crankcase of compressors. They are typically installed in refrigeration and air conditioning systems to maintain the temperature of the crankcase above the dew point, which in turn prevents the formation of oil buildup and possible damage to the compressor.</p><p>The crankcase heaters market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing adoption of refrigeration and air conditioning systems in sectors such as residential, commercial, and industrial is a major driving factor for the market.
