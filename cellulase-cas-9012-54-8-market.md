<p><p>Cellulase (CAS 9012-54-8) is an enzyme that aids in the breakdown of cellulose, a complex carbohydrate found in plant cell walls. It has various market types based on its specific activities. Endoglucanase (EG) market involves the hydrolysis of internal β-1,4-glycosidic bonds in cellulose. Cellobiohydrolase (CBH) market focuses on cleaving cellobiose units from the cellulose chain ends. β-Glucosidase (BG) market deals with the final breakdown of cellobiose into glucose. These different market types of cellulase cater to specific industrial applications, such as biofuel production, textile processing, and food and beverage industries.</p></p>
