<p><p>Pipette stands are essential tools used in laboratories to securely hold pipettes. There are two main types of pipette stands available in the market: rotating and fixed stands. Rotating stands allow for easy access and rotation of pipettes, enabling quick selection and retrieval. They are often used in busy laboratories where multiple pipettes need to be readily available. On the other hand, fixed stands are designed to hold pipettes securely in an upright position. They are commonly used in laboratories where pipettes are less frequently used and require stability and organization.</p></p>
