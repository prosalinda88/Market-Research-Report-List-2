<p><p>The Multiple Listing Service (MLS) software market consists of two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based MLS software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access it through the internet without the need for physical installation. It offers scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, on-premises MLS software is installed directly on local servers and requires physical maintenance and management. It provides better data control but may require higher upfront costs and limited scalability. Both types provide real estate professionals with tools to manage property listings, searches, and collaborations efficiently.</p></p>
