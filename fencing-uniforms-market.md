<p><p>The fencing uniforms market consists of three main types: foil kits, epee kits, and sabre kits. Foil kits are specifically designed for the sport of foil fencing, which involves thrusting and scoring points with a flexible blade. Epee kits cater to epee fencing, known for its rigid blade and the ability to score points with any part of the body. Sabre kits are intended for sabre fencing, where points are scored with cutting or thrusting using a specialized weapon. Each kit is tailored to the specific needs and regulations of the respective fencing discipline.</p></p>
