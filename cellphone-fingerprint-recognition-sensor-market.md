<p><p>The cellphone fingerprint recognition sensor market is divided into several types. Capacitance sensors use electrical current to detect the unique ridges and valleys of a fingerprint. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to create a detailed 3D image of the fingerprint. Optical sensors capture an image of the fingerprint using light and then analyze it. RF sensors use radio frequency to read the fingerprint and create a digital representation. These different sensor types offer various approaches to secure and convenient fingerprint recognition on cellphones.</p></p>
