<p><p>The Brain Natriuretic Peptide (BNP) Test is a blood test used to measure the levels of BNP in the bloodstream. BNP is a hormone secreted by the heart in response to increased pressure on heart muscles, and it acts as a diuretic and vasodilator. The test is primarily used to diagnose and monitor heart failure and other cardiac conditions. Elevated levels of BNP indicate heart dysfunction, while low levels indicate a healthy heart.</p><p>The Brain Natriuretic Peptide (BNP) Test Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
