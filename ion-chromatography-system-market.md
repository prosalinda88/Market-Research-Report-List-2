<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ion Chromatography System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange Chromatography</li><li>Ion Exclusion Chromatography</li><li>Ion Pair Chromatography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ion chromatography system market includes three major types: ion exchange chromatography, ion exclusion chromatography, and ion pair chromatography. </p><p>1. Ion exchange chromatography separates ions based on their charge, using a stationary phase with charged functional groups that attract or exchange ions.</p><p>2. Ion exclusion chromatography separates ions based on their size, with a stationary phase that allows only small molecules to enter, excluding larger ions.</p><p>3. Ion pair chromatography separates charged ions by adding a suitable counter ion to form a neutral ion pair, allowing easier separation based on size or charge.</p></p>
