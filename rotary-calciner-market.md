<p><strong>The Rotary Calciner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ordinary Rotary Calciner</li><li>Indirect Rotary Calciner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary calciner market can be categorized into two types: ordinary rotary calciners and indirect rotary calciners. Ordinary rotary calciners directly heat materials, such as limestone or clay, causing them to undergo chemical reactions. These calciners are commonly used in the cement industry for the production of cement clinker. On the other hand, indirect rotary calciners use an external burner to heat the material indirectly, leading to controlled and more efficient calcination processes. These calciners find applications in various industries including chemicals, minerals, and food processing.</p></p>
