<p><p>Lactic acid drinks can be categorized into two types: traditional lactic acid drinks and cultured lactic acid drinks. Traditional lactic acid drinks are produced by spontaneous fermentation of carbohydrates, such as fruits or vegetables. These drinks have a sour taste and contain various beneficial bacteria. On the other hand, cultured lactic acid drinks are made by deliberately adding specific strains of bacteria to the fermentation process. These drinks are known for their probiotic properties and are often marketed as a healthy option for gut health.</p></p>
