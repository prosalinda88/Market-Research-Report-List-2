<p><p>Orbital welding is a specialized technique used in various industries for high-quality and precise welding applications. The orbital welding market consists of different types, including Gas Tungsten Arc Welding (GTAW), Gas Metal Arc Welding (GMAW), Flux Core Arc Welding (FCAW), and others. GTAW utilizes a tungsten electrode and an inert gas to create the weld. GMAW employs a consumable metal electrode and shielding gas. FCAW uses a flux-cored wire electrode that shields the weld. The "Others" category includes other welding methods like plasma arc welding, laser welding, and electron beam welding.</p></p>
