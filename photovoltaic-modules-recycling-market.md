<p><p>The photovoltaic modules recycling market consists of three main types: monocrystalline photovoltaic modules, polycrystalline photovoltaic modules, and thin-film modules. Monocrystalline photovoltaic modules are made from a single crystal structure and have high efficiency. Polycrystalline photovoltaic modules are made from multiple crystal structures and are generally less expensive. Thin-film modules use a thin semiconductor layer and are flexible, making them suitable for various applications.
