<p><p>A Helium Ion Microscope (HIM) is an advanced imaging tool that utilizes a helium ion beam instead of the traditional electron beam found in conventional electron microscopes. The key advantage of HIM is its ability to produce high-resolution images while minimizing sample damage. HIMs employ a helium ion beam with a smaller de Broglie wavelength than electrons, allowing for the visualization of fine surface details with sub-nanometer resolution. </p><p>The Helium Ion Microscopes Market has been witnessing significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue its upward trajectory in the forecast period. The market is being driven by several factors, including increasing research and development activities in various industries such as semiconductors, materials science, and pharmaceuticals.
