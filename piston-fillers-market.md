<p><p>Piston Fillers market consists of two main types: Fully-automatic and Semi-automatic. Fully-automatic piston fillers are designed to automatically fill containers with precise amounts of liquid or semi-viscous products, eliminating the need for manual intervention. They are ideal for high-volume production environments where efficiency and accuracy are paramount. On the other hand, Semi-automatic piston fillers require manual loading and unloading of containers but automate the filling process, allowing for increased productivity and consistent fill volumes. These are commonly used in smaller-scale operations or when cost considerations are important.</p></p>
