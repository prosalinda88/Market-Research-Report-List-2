<p><p>A thermography machine, also known as an infrared camera or thermal imaging camera, is a device that captures and creates images based on the thermal radiation emitted by objects. It uses infrared sensors to detect temperature variations and convert them into a visible image, thus allowing users to detect anomalies or irregularities in temperature distribution that cannot be seen by the naked eye. The applications of thermography machines are diverse, ranging from industrial inspections and building diagnostics to healthcare and veterinary medicine.</p><p>The thermography machine market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
