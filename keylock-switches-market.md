<p><p>Keylock switches are a type of switch commonly used in various industries and applications. There are two main types - single-pole and double-pole keylock switches. Single-pole keylock switches have one set of contacts, allowing for the control of a single circuit. On the other hand, double-pole keylock switches have two separate sets of contacts, enabling the control of two circuits simultaneously. Both types of switches offer enhanced security and access control by requiring a key to be inserted and turned in order to operate the switch.</p></p>
