<p><p>The enclosed belt conveyor market consists of three main types: roller, spool, and air cushion. The roller system utilizes cylindrical rollers to support and move the belt, ensuring smooth and efficient transportation. The spool type employs spools to support the belt, offering flexibility in installation and space utilization. Meanwhile, the air cushion type utilizes a cushion of air to support and transport the belt, resulting in decreased friction and energy consumption. These different types cater to various industries' needs for enclosed belt conveyors, providing suitable solutions for efficient material handling.</p></p>
