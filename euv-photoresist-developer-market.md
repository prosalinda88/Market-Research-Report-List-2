<p><strong>The EUV Photoresist Developer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist Developer</li><li>Negative Photoresist Developer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EUV photoresist developer market consists of two types: positive photoresist developer and negative photoresist developer. Positive photoresist developer is used to remove the exposed areas of the photoresist film, leaving behind the unexposed areas. It is commonly employed in the production of advanced microprocessors. On the other hand, negative photoresist developer is utilized to dissolve the unexposed regions, leaving behind the exposed regions. This type is commonly used in applications such as fabrication of microelectromechanical systems (MEMS) and high-performance displays.</p></p>
