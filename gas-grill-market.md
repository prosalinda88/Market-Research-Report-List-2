<p><p>The gas grill market is comprised of two main types: propane gas grills and natural gas grills. Propane gas grills are fueled by propane gas tanks, which offer convenience as they can be easily replaced. They are also portable, making them suitable for outdoor activities. On the other hand, natural gas grills are connected to a home's natural gas supply, ensuring a constant fuel source. They are typically larger and more expensive, predominantly used in permanent outdoor installations. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and requirements.</p></p>
