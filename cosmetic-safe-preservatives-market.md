<p><p>The cosmetic safe preservatives market consists of various types of preservatives that ensure the safety and longevity of cosmetic products. These types include paraben esters, formaldehyde donors, phenol derivatives, alcohols, inorganics, quaternary compounds, and others. Paraben esters are widely used due to their effectiveness against a broad range of microorganisms. Formaldehyde donors release formaldehyde over time to prevent bacterial growth. Phenol derivatives inhibit the growth of fungi and bacteria. Alcohols act as antimicrobial agents. Inorganics, such as organic acids and salts, possess antimicrobial properties.
