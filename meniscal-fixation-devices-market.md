<p><p>Meniscal fixation devices are surgical instruments used in the treatment of meniscal tear or injury. The market offers various types of these devices, including arrows, screws, darts, and others. Arrows are small implantable devices made of absorbable materials that help secure the meniscus to the bone. Screws are metallic devices used to fix the meniscus and stabilize it during the healing process. Darts are specialized devices that penetrate the meniscus to hold it in place. Other types may include sutures, staples, or anchors, each serving to hold the meniscal tissue securely.</p></p>
