<p><p>The dried yeast market encompasses two main types: active dry yeast and inactive dry yeast. Active dry yeast is a form of yeast that is dormant and requires activation before use. It is commonly used in home baking and has a longer shelf life. On the other hand, inactive dry yeast, also known as nutritional yeast, is a deactivated form of yeast. It is rich in essential nutrients, particularly B vitamins, and is commonly used as a supplement or flavor enhancer in various foods, such as vegetarian and vegan dishes.</p></p>
