<p><p>Appendage management refers to the various devices used for preventing blood clots in the left atrial appendage (LAA) of the heart. There are two main types of devices in the market: epicardial LAA closure devices and endocardial LAA closure devices. Epicardial devices are placed on the outside of the heart and seal off the LAA, while endocardial devices are inserted through a catheter and close off the LAA from the inside. Both devices aim to reduce the risk of stroke by preventing clot formation in the LAA.</p></p>
