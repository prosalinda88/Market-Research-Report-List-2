<p><p>Variable Frequency Drive (VFD) market is divided into three major types: AC Drives, DC Drives, and Servo Drives. AC Drives, also known as adjustable speed drives, control the speed and torque of an AC electric motor, resulting in energy savings and improved motor performance. DC Drives are used to control the speed of DC motors, providing precise speed control and smooth operation. Servo Drives are designed for high-performance motion control applications, offering precise positioning and speed control for servo motors. Each type caters to different motor control needs and applications.</p></p>
