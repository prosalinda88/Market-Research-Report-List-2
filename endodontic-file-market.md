<p><ul><li>Stainless Steel Endodontic File</li><li>Nickel-Titanium Endodontic File</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The endodontic file market consists of two main types: stainless steel endodontic files and nickel-titanium endodontic files. Stainless steel files are made of a durable and corrosion-resistant material, often used for initial shaping and cleaning of root canals. Nickel-titanium files, on the other hand, are more flexible and have shape memory, allowing for easier navigation in curved canals and reducing the risk of instrument fracture. Both types are essential tools in endodontic procedures, providing dentists with different options based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
