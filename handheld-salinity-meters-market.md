<p><p>Handheld salinity meters are portable devices used to measure the salt concentration in various solutions. The market for these meters is categorized into three types: single channel, dual channel, and multi-channel. </p><p>Single channel meters are equipped with one measurement channel, allowing users to measure salinity in one sample at a time. </p><p>Dual channel meters have two measurement channels, enabling simultaneous measurement of salinity in two different samples. This feature offers greater efficiency and convenience for users. </p><p>Multi-channel meters have multiple measurement channels, allowing simultaneous measurement of salinity in multiple samples. This type is ideal for users dealing with a higher volume of samples or requiring faster analysis.</p></p>
