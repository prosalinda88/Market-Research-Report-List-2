<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Gyroscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Interferometric</li><li>Resonant</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical fiber gyroscope market types include interferometric, resonant, and other markets. Interferometric gyroscopes utilize the Sagnac effect to measure angular velocity by splitting a laser beam into two paths and then recombining them to create interference. Resonant gyroscopes use the principle of mechanical resonance to detect angular velocity, with a vibrating structure providing the sensing element. Other market types incorporate different sensing mechanisms and technologies. These variations in optical fiber gyroscope market types offer different levels of performance, accuracy, and application suitability to cater to diverse requirements in various industries.</p></p>
