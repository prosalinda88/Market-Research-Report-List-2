<p><p>Barium-based grease is a type of lubricant that is formulated by mixing a base oil with a barium soap thickener. Barium-based grease offers excellent oxidative stability, mechanical stability, and shear stability, making it suitable for a wide range of applications.</p><p>The future outlook of the barium-based grease market is expected to be positive.
