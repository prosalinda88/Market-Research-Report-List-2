<p><p>Asphalt pavers are machines used in the construction industry to lay asphalt on roads, parking lots, and other surfaces. They are utilized for efficiently and accurately spreading asphalt mixtures, ensuring a smooth and durable pavement. Asphalt pavers consist of a hopper that holds the asphalt mixture, a conveyor system that delivers the mixture to the distribution auger, and a screed that levels and compacts the asphalt.</p><p>The global asphalt pavers market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 5.6% during the forecast period. The primary drivers for this market growth include increasing road construction activities, infrastructure development projects, and the rise in urbanization.
