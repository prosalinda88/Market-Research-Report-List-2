<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>RO water dispenser market is divided into two types: Point of Use (POU) and Point of Entry (POE). POU RO water dispensers are directly connected to the water source and purify the water before dispensing it. These are ideal for small residential or office environments. On the other hand, POE RO water dispensers are installed at the main water supply point, ensuring all water in the entire building is purified. This type is suitable for large residential complexes, commercial buildings, or industries where a centralized purification system is required.</p></p>
