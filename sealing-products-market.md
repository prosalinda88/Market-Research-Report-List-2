<p><p>Sealing products refer to a range of items used to prevent leakage and provide a secure seal in various industries. General gaskets are versatile and commonly used in pipelines and machinery. Gasket sheets are large, flat gaskets made from materials like rubber or fiberglass. Metallic and semimetallic gaskets are designed to withstand high temperatures and pressures. Elastomer packings, such as O-rings, are made from rubber-like materials and provide flexible compression seals. Fluoropolymer components, such as PTFE and PFA, offer excellent chemical resistance. The packing market includes all these types of sealing products.</p></p>
