<p><strong>The Oxygen Plants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cryogenic Distillation</li><li>Molecular Sieve Adsorption Method</li><li>Membrane Permeation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Oxygen Plants Market is categorized into three types: Cryogenic Distillation, Molecular Sieve Adsorption Method, and Membrane Permeation. Cryogenic Distillation plants separate oxygen from air by cooling it to extremely low temperatures, condensing the oxygen and removing impurities. The Molecular Sieve Adsorption Method utilizes a molecular sieve to adsorb impurities and allow oxygen to pass through. In Membrane Permeation plants, a selectively permeable membrane separates oxygen from other gases based on their diffusion rates. These different methods provide diverse options for generating oxygen efficiently in various industries.</p></p>
