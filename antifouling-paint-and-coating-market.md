<p><p>Antifouling paint and coating refer to a type of protective coating applied to surfaces of ships, boats, and other marine vessels to prevent the growth of organisms such as barnacles, algae, and mussels. Antifouling paint and coating contain biocides or other chemicals that deter the growth of such organisms, thereby minimizing the need for regular cleaning or maintenance.</p><p>The future outlook for the antifouling paint and coating market is promising.
