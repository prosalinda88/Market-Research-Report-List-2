<p><ul><li>Photon Detector</li><li>Thermal Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-speed photodetector market consists of two main types: photon detector and thermal detector. Photon detectors are designed to directly detect and convert photons into electrical signals. They are commonly used in applications such as optical communication and imaging systems. On the other hand, thermal detectors measure changes in temperature caused by the absorption of photons, generating electrical signals. These detectors are utilized in various applications, including temperature measurement and infrared imaging. Both types play crucial roles in enabling high-speed photodetection in different fields and industries.</p></p>
