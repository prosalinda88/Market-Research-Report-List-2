<p><p>Ultrasonic humidifiers are divided into two market types - tabletop and portable. Tabletop humidifiers are designed to be placed on a flat surface, such as a table or countertop, and are typically used indoors in small to medium-sized rooms. They provide localized humidification and often come with additional features like adjustable mist levels. On the other hand, portable humidifiers are compact and lightweight, allowing for easy transport and use in various locations. These models are ideal for travel or moving between different rooms or areas within a household.</p></p>
