<p><p>Chiropractic care is a form of alternative medicine that focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of musculoskeletal disorders, particularly related to the spine. The market is primarily fueled by the rising prevalence of musculoskeletal disorders, such as back pain and neck pain, which are often caused by sedentary lifestyles, poor posture, and physical injuries. Additionally, the growing awareness about the benefits of chiropractic care, coupled with advancements in technology and treatment techniques, has further contributed to market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the chiropractic care market has been witnessing a shift towards integrated healthcare approaches, where chiropractors work in collaboration with other healthcare professionals to provide comprehensive and holistic patient care.
