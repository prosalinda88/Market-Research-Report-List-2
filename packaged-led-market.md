This has led to the widespread adoption of Packaged LED across various industries, including residential, commercial, industrial, automotive, and healthcare.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing demand for display and backlighting applications in consumer electronics, such as smartphones, tablets, and televisions, has further fueled the growth of the Packaged LED Market. The rising trend of miniaturization and lightweight design in electronic devices has propelled the demand for smaller and more efficient LED packages.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a growing preference for organic light-emitting diode (OLED) technology, which offers several advantages over traditional LED.
