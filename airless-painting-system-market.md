<p><p>The airless painting system market is categorized into three types: electric airless painting system, gas airless painting system, and others. An electric airless painting system utilizes an electric motor to operate a piston which pressurizes the paint, providing a smooth and uniform application. On the other hand, a gas airless painting system uses a gas-powered engine to generate pressure for paint expulsion. Lastly, the "others" category includes air-assisted airless systems, which combine both airless and conventional spraying technologies for high-quality paint finishes.</p></p>
