<p><p>The electrosurgical products market comprises various types of equipment used in surgical procedures. Electrosurgical generators are devices that provide the necessary electrical energy to perform the procedure. Handpieces are instruments that connect to the generator and allow precise control over the surgical process. Accessories encompass additional tools and devices that aid in the procedure, such as cords and foot switches. Consumables include disposable items like electrodes and blades that need to be replaced after each surgery. These products collectively play a vital role in ensuring safe and successful electrosurgical procedures.</p></p>
