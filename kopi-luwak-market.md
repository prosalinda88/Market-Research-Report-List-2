As the traditional method of harvesting civet coffee beans involves capturing wild civets and force-feeding them with coffee cherries, there has been a shift towards more ethical practices. This has led to the emergence of coffee plantations and farmers who allow civets to consume coffee cherries naturally in their habitats, ensuring the beans are collected directly from their droppings.</p><p>Moreover, the market is witnessing innovative product launches and marketing strategies.
