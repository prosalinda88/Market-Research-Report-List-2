<p><p>Heat sterilization equipment market includes two main types: dry heat sterilization equipment and moist heat sterilization equipment. Dry heat sterilization equipment uses high temperatures to kill microorganisms, including spores. It is suitable for materials that are heat-resistant but moisture-sensitive. On the other hand, moist heat sterilization equipment uses steam under high pressure to sterilize materials. It is particularly effective against microorganisms that are heat and moisture-resistant. Both types play a crucial role in various industries, such as healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and food processing, ensuring proper sterilization and preventing the transmission of harmful infections.</p></p>
