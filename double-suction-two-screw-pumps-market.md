<p><p>Double-suction two-screw pumps are positive displacement pumps that are used for pumping fluids with high viscosity, including oils, crude oil, and other hydrocarbons. These pumps have two parallel mounted screws with opposing thread directions, which rotate in the pump casing to create pressure and move the fluid. The fluid flows from both ends of the pump, resulting in a double-suction design that enhances efficiency and reduces axial forces.</p><p>The double-suction two-screw pumps market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
