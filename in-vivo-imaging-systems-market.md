<p><p>In Vivo Imaging Systems Market Types refer to different imaging techniques used in medical and research fields to capture real-time images of living organisms. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) uses magnetic fields and radio waves to produce detailed images of internal structures. Digital Angiography examines blood vessels through X-ray imaging. Ultrasound employs high-frequency sound waves to create images of internal organs. Nuclear Imaging involves the use of radioactive substances to visualize specific areas or functions. Optical Imaging (OI) employs light to create images on a molecular level. "Others" include various emerging imaging technologies not listed above.</p></p>
