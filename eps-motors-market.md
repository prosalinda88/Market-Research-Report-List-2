<p><strong>The EPS Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS Motor</li><li>REPS Motor</li><li>PEPS Motor</li><li>EHPS Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EPS motors refer to Electric Power Steering motors, which are used in vehicles to assist with steering. There are four types of EPS motors: CEPS, REPS, PEPS, and EHPS. CEPS (Column Electric Power Steering) motors are located in the steering column, REPS (Rack Electric Power Steering) motors are found in the steering rack, PEPS (Pinion Electric Power Steering) motors are located in the steering pinion, and EHPS (Electrically Hydraulic Power Steering) motors combine electric and hydraulic power steering mechanisms. These different market types cater to various vehicle models and manufacturers' specifications.</p></p>
