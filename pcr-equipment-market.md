<p><ul><li>Real Time PCR Equipment</li><li>Standard PCR Equipment</li><li>Digital PCR Equipment</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCR equipment market comprises different types of equipment used in polymerase chain reaction (PCR) technology. Real-time PCR equipment enables the monitoring of DNA amplification in real-time, providing accurate and quantitative results. Standard PCR equipment is used for traditional PCR analysis without real-time monitoring. Digital PCR equipment is designed to precisely quantify DNA samples by partitioning them into many individual reactions. The "others" category includes specialized PCR equipment, such as thermal cyclers with advanced features or instruments used specifically for reverse transcription PCR or multiplex PCR.</p></p>
