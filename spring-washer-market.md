<p><p>A spring washer, also known as a lock washer, is a fastener that is used to prevent loosening or vibration in bolted or nutted joints. It is a small circular metal washer with a centrally located hole and a slight conical shape. When the bolt or nut is tightened, the spring washer flattens and creates a tensioned spring force between the fastener and the joint, effectively preventing any loosening due to vibrations.</p><p>The Spring Washer Market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for spring washers from various industries such as automotive, construction, aerospace, and machinery is driving market growth. The automotive industry, in particular, is a major consumer of spring washers due to the high usage of bolts and nuts in vehicle assembly.</p><p>Another factor contributing to the market growth is the rising awareness about the importance of fasteners in ensuring the safety and reliability of mechanical assemblies.
