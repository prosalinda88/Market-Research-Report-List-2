<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Waveguide Grating Router Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Type</li><li>Athermal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waveguide Grating Router market can be categorized into two types - Thermal Type and Athermal Type. </p><p>A Thermal Type Waveguide Grating Router relies on temperature variations to adjust the wavelength. It utilizes materials with different thermal expansion coefficients to achieve wavelength selectivity, which can limit its stability and reliability in certain applications.</p><p>On the other hand, an Athermal Type Waveguide Grating Router is designed to independently adapt to environmental temperature changes. It employs various compensating techniques to counteract temperature-induced wavelength drift, ensuring stable and accurate performance regardless of temperature fluctuations. This makes it suitable for applications where precise wavelength control is crucial.</p></p>
