<p><p>The dispersive prisms market offers various types of prisms, including the 30°-60°-90° right angle prism and equilateral prisms. The 30°-60°-90° right angle prism consists of a right triangle shape, with one angle measuring 90 degrees and the other two angles measuring 30 and 60 degrees. Equilateral prisms have three equal angles of 60 degrees and flat sides. These prisms are commonly used in optics and spectroscopy applications to disperse and manipulate light, allowing for the study of different wavelengths and colors.</p></p>
