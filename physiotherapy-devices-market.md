<p><p>The physiotherapy devices market includes two main types: equipment and accessories. Equipment refers to the larger devices used in physiotherapy treatments, such as ultrasound machines, electric muscle stimulators, and continuous passive motion machines. These devices are designed to assist in the rehabilitation and recovery process. On the other hand, accessories are the smaller components or add-ons used with the equipment, like electrodes, gel pads, and transducers.
