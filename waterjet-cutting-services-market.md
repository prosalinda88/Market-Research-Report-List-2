<p><ul><li>Pure Water Jet Cutting</li><li>Abrasive Water Jet Cutting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waterjet cutting services are widely used in various industries for precise cutting of materials. Two main types of waterjet cutting services are pure water jet cutting and abrasive water jet cutting. </p><p>Pure water jet cutting uses a high-pressure stream of pure water to cut softer materials like foam, rubber, and textiles, as it does not require added abrasives. On the other hand, abrasive water jet cutting involves a mixture of water and abrasive particles, such as garnet, to cut harder materials like metal and stone. The addition of abrasives enhances the cutting power and allows for more precise and efficient cutting.</p></p>
