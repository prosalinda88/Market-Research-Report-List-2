<p><p>The molten salt solar energy thermal storage market includes various types, such as Tower-type Solar Power Tower Systems, Dish Concentrating Solar Power Systems, and Other markets. Tower-type systems use a tall tower surrounded by heliostat mirrors to reflect sunlight onto a receiver at the tower's top. Dish concentrating systems use a parabolic dish to concentrate sunlight onto a receiver at the dish's focal point. Other markets may include alternative technologies like linear Fresnel systems or solar chimney power plants. Each type harnesses sun's energy and uses molten salt as a storage medium to produce electricity.</p></p>
