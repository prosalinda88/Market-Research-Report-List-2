<p><ul><li>Single Piece Rail Gangways</li><li>Two Piece Rail Gangways</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rail gangways market consists of two primary types: single piece rail gangways and two piece rail gangways. Single piece rail gangways are made of a single continuous piece of material, providing a seamless connection between rail vehicles. On the other hand, two piece rail gangways are composed of two separate sections, allowing for flexibility in coupling and uncoupling rail cars. Both types play a crucial role in establishing safe and secure passage for passengers and crew during boarding and disembarking processes in the railway industry.</p></p>
