<p><p>Building damping products refer to various types of products used in construction to reduce the effects of vibrations and oscillations caused by external forces on buildings. Viscous and visco-elastic dampers utilize viscous fluids or viscoelastic materials to absorb and dissipate energy. Tuned mass dampers are specific devices designed to counteract building vibrations. Tuned liquid dampers use liquid containers to reduce building sway. Liquid dampers employ fluids to mitigate oscillations. The "others" category includes various damping systems not classified in the above types, such as friction dampers and electronic dampers.</p></p>
