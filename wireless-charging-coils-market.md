Wireless charging coils with up to 10 uH inductance are suitable for smaller electronic devices, while those with 10 to 15 uH inductance are ideal for medium-sized devices. Coils with 15 to 20 uH inductance are commonly used in larger devices. These categories help ensure efficient and reliable wireless charging for a wide range of devices.</p></p>
