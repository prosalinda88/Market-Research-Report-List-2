<p><p>Data colocation services refer to the practice of renting physical space, infrastructure, and resources to businesses for housing their data servers and networking equipment. Retail colocation services target smaller businesses and offer them smaller space options, with additional services like connectivity and support. Wholesale colocation services, on the other hand, cater to larger businesses and provide them with larger spaces, often spanning entire data centers, without the additional services. Both types facilitate businesses in securely storing their data offsite, ensuring reliable connectivity and reducing costs associated with building and maintaining their own data centers.</p></p>
