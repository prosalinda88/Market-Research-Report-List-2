<p><ul><li>Dry Pectin</li><li>Liquid Pectin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Apple Pectin market consists of two main types: Dry Pectin and Liquid Pectin. Dry Pectin is a powdered form derived from apples, commonly used as a thickening agent in food products. It provides a longer shelf life due to its low moisture content and is preferred for making jams, jellies, and baked goods. On the other hand, Liquid Pectin is a fluid extract that offers convenience in food preparations. It is used when immediate and even dispersion is required, often found in products like yogurts, ice creams, and sauces.</p></p>
