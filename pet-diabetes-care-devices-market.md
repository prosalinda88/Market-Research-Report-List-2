Changing lifestyles, feeding habits, and increasing obesity rates among pets are contributing to the prevalence of diabetes. As people become more aware of pet healthcare, they are more likely to invest in devices that help manage chronic conditions like diabetes in their pets.</p><p>Additionally, advancements in technology and the development of user-friendly devices are boosting the market. <p><p>The pet diabetes care devices market includes two main types: insulin delivery pens and insulin syringes. These pens come with pre-filled insulin cartridges and offer adjustable dosage options. Both devices play a crucial role in managing diabetes in pets and ensuring proper insulin administration for effective treatment.</p></p>
