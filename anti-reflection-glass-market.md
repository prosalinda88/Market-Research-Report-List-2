<p><p>Anti-reflection glass is available in different types - plate glass and deep-processed glass. Plate glass is a type of anti-reflection glass that is manufactured using a rolling process and consists of a flat transparent sheet. Deep-processed glass, on the other hand, undergoes additional treatments, such as tempering or laminating, to enhance its strength and durability. Both plate glass and deep-processed glass are used for applications that require reduced reflection, such as optical instruments, display panels, and solar panels, providing clearer and more efficient visibility.</p></p>
