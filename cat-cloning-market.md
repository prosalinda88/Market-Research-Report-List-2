<p><p>Cat cloning refers to the process of creating genetically identical copies of a cat through a scientific procedure called somatic cell nuclear transfer (SCNT). This technique involves removing the nucleus of an unfertilized egg and replacing it with the nucleus of a somatic cell from the cat to be cloned. The egg is then stimulated to develop into an embryo and transferred to a recipient female cat, who carries the pregnancy to term.</p><p>The cat cloning market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 20.00% during the forecast period. Firstly, cloning allows cat owners to preserve the genetic material of their beloved feline companions, even after they have passed away. This emotional attachment drives demand for cloning services.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in genetic technology and the decreasing cost of cloning procedures have made it more accessible to a broader range of individuals.
