Digital number grip dynamometers display the grip strength directly on a digital screen. Digital recording dial grip dynamometers not only provide a digital display but also record the measurements for future analysis. Manual recording dial grip dynamometers have a traditional dial that displays the grip strength, and users manually record the readings. These different types cater to the varying needs and preferences of users when it comes to tracking and analyzing grip strength.</p></p>
