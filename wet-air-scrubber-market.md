<p><p>The wet air scrubber market includes different types of air scrubbers, such as 3-stage and 4-stage air scrubbers. A 3-stage air scrubber is a pollution control device that uses a three-step process to remove pollutants from the air. It typically consists of a pre-scrubber, a wet scrubber, and a mist eliminator. On the other hand, a 4-stage air scrubber incorporates an additional stage, often called an absorber, which helps remove specific types of pollutants more effectively. Both types of air scrubbers are commonly used in various industries to improve air quality and comply with environmental regulations.</p></p>
