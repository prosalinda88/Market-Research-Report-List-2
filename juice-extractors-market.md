<p><strong>The Juice Extractors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Juicer</li><li>Masticating Juicer</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Juice extractors are categorized into different types based on their mechanism and features. Centrifugal juicers work by using high-speed spinning blades that extract juice quickly but may generate heat and produce less nutritious juice. Masticating juicers use a slower, chewing-like action to extract juice, preserving more nutrients and producing a higher juice yield. Other types of juice extractors include citrus juicers, manual juicers, and hybrid juicers that combine different extraction methods. These variations allow consumers to choose a juicer that aligns with their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
