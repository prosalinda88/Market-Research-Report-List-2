<p><p>HVAC Damper Actuator market consists of two main types: Non-Spring Return Damper Actuators and Spring Return Damper Actuators. Non-Spring Return Damper Actuators are electronic devices that control the opening and closing of HVAC dampers using electric signals. They provide constant control and precise positioning. In contrast, Spring Return Damper Actuators use springs to move the damper back to its default position in case of power loss. They are cost-effective and require less power. Both types play a vital role in regulating airflow and ensuring efficient HVAC system performance.</p></p>
