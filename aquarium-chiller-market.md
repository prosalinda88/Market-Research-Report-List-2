<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The aquarium chiller market can be categorized into three types: inline water chillers, drop-in chillers, and multi-temp chillers. Inline water chillers are installed in the aquarium's plumbing system to cool the water as it circulates through. Drop-in chillers are standalone units placed directly into the aquarium to cool the water. Multi-temp chillers are advanced systems that allow users to set different temperatures in different sections of the aquarium, catering to the needs of various aquatic species. These types provide efficient cooling options for maintaining ideal water temperature in aquariums.</p></p>
