<p><p>The dicing die attach film adhesives market is categorized into two types: two-component and single-component adhesives. Two-component adhesives consist of two parts, a resin and a hardener, which need to be mixed before application. These adhesives offer excellent bonding strength and longer shelf life. On the other hand, single-component adhesives are ready-to-use and do not require any additional mixing. They provide convenience and ease of application but may have shorter shelf life compared to two-component adhesives.</p></p>
