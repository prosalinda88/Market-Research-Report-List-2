<p><p>The System on Chip (SoC) market can be categorized into four types: digital, analog, mixed-signal, and others. The digital SoC includes integrated circuits that process digital signals. Analog SoC consists of circuits that process continuous signals, such as audio or temperature. Mixed-signal SoC combines both digital and analog circuits to process both types of signals. The "others" category includes specialized SoC types like programmable SoCs or application-specific integrated circuits (ASICs), which cater to specific functions or industries. These different types cater to varying requirements and applications in the SoC market.</p></p>
