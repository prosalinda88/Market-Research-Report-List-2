<p><p>Polyalkylene Glycol (PAG)-based grease can be categorized into two types based on their solubility in water. The first type is not soluble in water, meaning it does not mix or dissolve in water. This type of PAG-based grease is ideal for applications where exposure to water is minimal or moisture resistance is required. The second type is soluble in water, which means it can mix and dissolve in water. This type is commonly used in applications where water contact or washout is a concern, allowing for easy removal or clean-up with water.</p></p>
