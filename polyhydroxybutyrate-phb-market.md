<p><p>Polyhydroxybutyrate (PHB) is a biodegradable thermoplastic polyester that is primarily used as a substitute for traditional petroleum-based plastics. It is produced by microorganisms through the fermentation of renewable carbon sources. There are different types of PHB, including PHB, PHBV, and PHBHx. PHB is a homopolymer consisting solely of hydroxybutyrate units. PHBV is a copolymer containing hydroxybutyrate and hydroxyvalerate units. PHBHx is also a copolymer but includes hydroxybutyrate and hydroxyhexanoate units. These different types offer varying properties and are used in a wide range of applications, including packaging, medical devices, and agricultural films.</p></p>
