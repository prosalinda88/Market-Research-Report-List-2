<p><p>The Power Hospital Bed Market primarily consists of two types: Full Electric Hospital Bed and Semi-electric Hospital Bed. Full Electric Hospital Beds are fully automated and can be adjusted for height, position, and backrest using an electric motor. They also have an electric feature to raise or lower the bed frame. On the other hand, Semi-electric Hospital Beds have manual adjustments for height and position, while the bed frame can be electrically adjusted. These beds provide convenience and comfort for patients and caregivers, improving patient care quality and ease of use.</p></p>
