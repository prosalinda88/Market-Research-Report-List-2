<p><p>A Volute Sludge Dewatering Machine is a type of sludge dewatering equipment used in various industries to efficiently separate solid and liquid phases of sludge. This causes the sludge to be compressed and dewatered as it moves towards the outlet.</p><p>The Volute Sludge Dewatering Machine Market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 13.7% during the forecast period.
