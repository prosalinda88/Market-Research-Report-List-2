<p><p>The hair wax market offers various types of waxes with unique properties. Beeswax, derived naturally from bees, provides a medium hold and is commonly used in hair products. Candelilla wax, sourced from candelilla shrubs, offers a firmer hold and is often used in vegan-friendly products. Carnauba wax, extracted from palm trees, provides a strong hold and a glossy finish. Castor wax, derived from castor oil, offers a thick and sticky texture. Emulsifying wax enables the blending of oil and water in products. Ozokerite is a mineral wax that provides a flexible hold. Lanolin, obtained from sheep's wool, moisturizes and adds texture to hair products.</p></p>
