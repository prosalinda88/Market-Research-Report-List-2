<p><ul><li>Three Rolls</li><li>Four Rolls</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High precision roll forming machines can be categorized into two main types: three rolls and four rolls. In three rolls roll forming machines, the material goes through three rolls to shape it into the desired form. These machines offer high precision and are typically suitable for producing complex profiles. On the other hand, four rolls roll forming machines utilize four rolls to shape the material, providing even higher accuracy and better control over the forming process. They are generally preferred for applications requiring tight tolerances and consistent results.</p></p>
