<p><p>The SMT SPI (Surface Mount Technology Solder Paste Inspection) equipment market offers two types of inspection systems: inline and offline. Inline SPI systems are integrated into the production line, allowing real-time inspection during the manufacturing process. They offer immediate feedback and enable quick adjustments to ensure quality output. On the other hand, offline SPI systems are standalone units used for post-production inspection. They are independent of the assembly line and provide comprehensive analysis of solder quality. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the accuracy and reliability of solder paste application in printed circuit board assembly.</p></p>
