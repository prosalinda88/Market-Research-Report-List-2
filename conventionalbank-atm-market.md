<p><ul><li>Deployment</li><li>Managed Services</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conventional/bank ATM market consists of two main types: deployment and managed services. Deployment involves banks or financial institutions purchasing and installing ATMs for their own use. They take care of the maintenance, cash replenishment, and customer service. On the other hand, managed services refer to outsourcing these functions to third-party providers. The service provider takes care of ATM deployment, maintenance, cash management, and customer support on behalf of the bank. This allows banks to focus on their core competencies while reducing costs and leveraging the expertise of the service provider.</p></p>
