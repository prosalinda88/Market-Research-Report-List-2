<p><strong>The Cell Sorting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluorescent Activated Cell Sorting</li><li>Magnetic-activated Cell Sorting</li><li>MEMS-Microfluidics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cell sorting market includes different types of technologies such as fluorescent-activated cell sorting (FACS), magnetic-activated cell sorting (MACS), and MEMS-microfluidics. FACS is a technique that uses fluorescence to identify and sort cells based on specific markers. MACS relies on the use of magnetic beads to label and separate target cells. MEMS-microfluidics combines microelectromechanical systems with microfluidic technology to sort cells based on physical properties. These technologies are used in various research areas, such as immunology and stem cell studies, to isolate and analyze specific cell populations for further investigation.</p></p>
