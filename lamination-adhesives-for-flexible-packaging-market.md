<p><strong>The Lamination Adhesives for Flexible Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based Adhesives</li><li>Solvent-free Adhesives</li><li>Water Based Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lamination adhesives for flexible packaging are used to join different layers of packaging materials together. Solvent-based adhesives are composed of solvents that evaporate during the bonding process, providing strong adhesion. Solvent-free adhesives do not contain solvents and offer a more environmentally friendly option, while still providing excellent bonding properties. Water-based adhesives are made up of water as a carrier and offer low VOC emissions, making them another sustainable choice. These different types of adhesives cater to varying needs and preferences in the flexible packaging market.</p></p>
