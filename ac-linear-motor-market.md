<p><p>An AC linear motor, also known as an alternating current linear motor, is an electric motor that operates on an AC power source to convert electrical energy into linear motion. Unlike traditional rotating motors, AC linear motors produce linear motion directly without the need for mechanical transmission systems such as belts or gears. These motors are widely used in various applications such as robotics, semiconductor manufacturing equipment, packaging machines, and machine tools.</p><p>The AC linear motor market has been experiencing significant growth due to the increasing demand for precise and efficient motion control systems across industries.
