<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Embedded Optical Fiber Polarizer market is categorized into two main types: SM Type and PM Type. SM Type stands for Single-Mode Type, which is designed to transmit a single-ray of light through the fiber. It is commonly used in long-distance telecommunication networks. On the other hand, PM Type refers to Polarization-Maintaining Type. This type of polarizer is specifically designed to maintain the polarization state of light signals as they travel through the fiber, ensuring better signal quality and avoiding signal degradation in applications like high-speed communications and fiber optic sensing systems.</p></p>
