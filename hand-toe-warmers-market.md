<p><p>Hand & Toe Warmers are products designed to keep hands and toes warm during cold weather or outdoor activities. There are several types of Hand & Toe Warmers in the market. Air activated Hand & Toe Warmers, also known as iron warmers, generate heat when iron powder reacts with oxygen in the air. Supersaturated solution warmers, or crystallisation-type warmers, use a liquid solution that crystallizes when activated, releasing heat. Lighter fuel warmers use a combustible fuel to generate heat, while battery warmers use batteries to produce heat. Apart from these, there are other types of Hand & Toe Warmers available in the market, each employing unique mechanisms to provide warmth and comfort.</p></p>
