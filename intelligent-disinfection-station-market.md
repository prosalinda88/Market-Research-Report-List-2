<p><p>The Intelligent Disinfection Station market comprises two main types: Dripping and Spray. Dripping disinfection stations release the disinfectant liquid in a slow and steady manner, allowing for thorough coverage and longer-lasting effects. This method is commonly used for surfaces and objects. On the other hand, Spray disinfection stations utilize a fine mist or spray to cover larger areas quickly. This method is often employed for disinfecting air and open spaces. Both types play a crucial role in providing efficient and effective disinfection solutions, each with its own advantages in different contexts.</p></p>
