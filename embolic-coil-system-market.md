<p><p>The Embolic Coil System is a medical device used for the treatment of cerebral aneurysms, which are weak areas in the blood vessels of the brain that may rupture and cause hemorrhagic strokes. This system consists of a platinum coil that is inserted into the aneurysm to block blood flow and promote clotting, thus preventing it from rupturing.</p><p>The Embolic Coil System Market is experiencing significant growth, primarily driven by the increasing prevalence of cerebral aneurysms and the rising demand for minimally invasive treatment options. The market is also being propelled by advancements in technology, such as the development of bioactive and biodegradable coils that provide better patient outcomes and reduce the risk of complications.</p><p>Moreover, the growing geriatric population, who are more prone to develop cerebral aneurysms, is expected to further contribute to market growth. Additionally, the rising awareness about early diagnosis and treatment of cerebrovascular diseases is fueling the adoption of embolic coil systems.</p><p>The market is witnessing several trends, including the shift towards detachable coils that offer enhanced flexibility and control during the procedure.
