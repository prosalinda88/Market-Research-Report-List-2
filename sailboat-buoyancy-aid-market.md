<p><p>The sailboat buoyancy aid market consists of three main types: overhead vests, front zip jackets, and side zippers. Overhead vests provide buoyancy and are worn over the head, offering unrestricted movement of the arms. Front zip jackets have a zipper on the front, allowing ease of wearing and removal. They offer good buoyancy and are commonly used in sailboat activities. Side zippers, as the name suggests, have zippers on the side for easy wearing. They offer similar buoyancy to front zip jackets and provide an alternative option for individuals based on personal preferences.</p></p>
