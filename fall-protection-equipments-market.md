<p><p>The fall protection equipments market comprises various types of safety gear designed to prevent falls and ensure worker safety. Harnesses, used around the torso, distribute the force of a fall across the body. Lanyards are attached to the harness and secure workers to anchor points. Self-retracting lifelines provide constant tension and retract quickly in case of a fall. Belts provide added support and stability. Other types of fall protection equipment include anchor points, carabiners, lifelines, and shock absorbers, all aimed at safeguarding workers at heights or in risky environments.</p></p>
