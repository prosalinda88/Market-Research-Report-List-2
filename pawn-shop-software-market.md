<p><ul><li>On-premise</li><li>Cloud-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pawn shop software is available in two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise software is installed directly on the pawn shop's own servers and computers, providing complete control and customization but requiring maintenance and updates. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It offers flexibility and scalability, with automatic software updates and data backups. Cloud-based solutions allow pawn shops to access their software from anywhere, enhancing collaboration and enabling seamless integration with other systems.</p></p>
