<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Optical Ground Wire (OPGW) is a type of cable used in the power transmission industry that combines optical fibers within the structure of a ground wire. The Central Tube Structure OPGW consists of an optical fiber bundle surrounded by a stainless steel tube and aluminum cladding. In contrast, the Layer Stranding Structure OPGW has multiple layers of stranded wires with optical fibers embedded in the outermost layer. Both types provide communication connectivity for power transmission lines, with the Layer Stranding Structure OPGW offering additional mechanical strength and improved optical performance.</p></p>
