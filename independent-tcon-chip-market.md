<p><p>The independent TCON chip market refers to the market for TCON chips that are used in various display applications. TCON chips are responsible for converting video signals into a format that can be displayed on the screen. There are different types of TCON interfaces, including LVDS (Low Voltage Differential Signaling), eDP (embedded DisplayPort), and others. LVDS is a popular interface that provides high-speed data transmission, while eDP is a newer and more advanced interface that offers higher resolution and better power efficiency. Other TCON interfaces may include HDMI, DVI, or MIPI, which are used in specific display applications.</p></p>
