<p><p>The powered surgical tools market encompasses three main types: electric-powered, battery-powered, and pneumatic-powered tools. Electric-powered tools rely on an electric power source for operation, providing consistent power output. Battery-powered tools, as the name suggests, use rechargeable batteries as their power source, offering enhanced portability. Pneumatic-powered tools utilize compressed air to generate their power, offering high power-to-weight ratios. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different surgical procedures and preferences.</p></p>
