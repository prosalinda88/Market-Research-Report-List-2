<p><p>The cold mixed asphalt additives market can be categorized into various types. Polymeric modifiers are materials that enhance the elastic properties and durability of cold mixed asphalt. Anti-strip and adhesion promoters improve the adhesion between asphalt and aggregates, preventing stripping. Emulsifiers facilitate the mixing of water with bitumen, making it suitable for cold mixing. Chemical modifiers enhance the workability and performance of cold mixed asphalt. Other types of additives include stabilizers, curing agents, and anti-oxidants, among others, which offer additional benefits in terms of performance and durability of cold mixed asphalt.</p></p>
