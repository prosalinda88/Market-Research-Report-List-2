<p><p>The cataract devices market includes several types of devices used in the treatment of cataracts. Intraocular lenses (IOLs) are artificial lenses implanted in the eye to replace the natural lens removed during cataract surgery. Ophthalmic viscosurgical devices (OVDs) are used to maintain intraocular space and protect delicate tissues during surgery. Phacoemulsification devices use ultrasound energy to break up and remove the cloudy lens. Femtosecond lasers offer precise and automated incisions during cataract surgery. These devices play a crucial role in the management of cataracts, a common vision-impairing condition.</p></p>
