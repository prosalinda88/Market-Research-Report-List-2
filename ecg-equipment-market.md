Monitoring Equipment refers to ECG devices used for continuous monitoring of heart activity. Holter Monitoring Systems are portable devices that record heart activity for a 24 to 48-hour period. Event Monitoring Systems are used to detect and record specific cardiac events. Stress Testing Systems are used to evaluate the heart's response to stress through exercises or medications.</p></p>
