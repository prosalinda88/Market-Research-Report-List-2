Threaded Connection valves are designed with threaded ends that can be easily screwed onto pipes or fittings. Welded Connection valves are connected through a welding process, ensuring a secure and leak-proof joint. Flanged Connection valves feature flanges that can be bolted together to connect them with pipelines.
