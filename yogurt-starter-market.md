<p><p>The yogurt starter market is divided into three main types: liquid yogurt starter, frozen yogurt starter, and direct throw yogurt starter. Liquid yogurt starter is a liquid form of bacteria and yeast cultures used to make yogurt. Frozen yogurt starter is a powdered or frozen form of cultures that needs to be rehydrated before use. Direct throw yogurt starter is a ready-to-use culture that can be directly added to the milk without any pre-treatment. Each type offers different convenience levels and storage requirements for consumers.</p></p>
