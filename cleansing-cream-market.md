<p><p>Cleansing cream is available in various market types, namely Water-in-Oil (W/O), Oil-in-Water (O/W), and Other markets. In the Water-in-Oil (W/O) market, the cream is formulated with water as the external phase and oil as the internal phase, providing a more moisturizing and nourishing effect. Conversely, in the Oil-in-Water (O/W) market, oil is the external phase and water is the internal phase, resulting in a lighter and more easily absorbed cream. Finally, the Other market comprises creams with different formulations and unique ingredients, catering to specific skincare needs or targeting specific skin concerns.</p></p>
