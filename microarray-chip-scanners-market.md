<p><p>The market for microarray chip scanners can be classified into two types: USB and IEEE. USB microarray chip scanners are equipped with a USB interface, enabling them to be connected directly to a computer for data transfer and analysis. On the other hand, IEEE microarray chip scanners use the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) communication standard to establish a connection with computers or other devices. Both types allow for efficient scanning and analysis of microarray chips, but differ in the way they connect to external devices.</p></p>
