<p><strong>The Powered Roof Supports Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chock Support</li><li>Shield Support</li><li>Chock Shield Support</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Powered Roof Supports market comprises three main types of support systems: Chock Support, Shield Support, and Chock Shield Support. Chock Support refers to individual hydraulic jacks that provide support to the roof in underground mines. Shield Support, on the other hand, consists of a complete set of hydraulic props and a shield canopy that offer protection to miners against roof falls. Lastly, Chock Shield Support combines the features of both chock and shield support systems, offering simultaneous roof support and roof fall protection.</p></p>
