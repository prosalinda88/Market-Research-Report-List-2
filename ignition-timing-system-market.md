<p><p>The ignition timing system market consists of two main types: timing belts and timing chains. Timing belts are made of rubber and have teeth that synchronize the crankshaft and camshaft rotation. They are quieter, lighter, and cheaper, but require regular replacement. On the other hand, timing chains are made of metal and use links to connect the crankshaft and camshaft. They are more durable and do not need frequent replacements but can be noisier and slightly heavier. Both types play a crucial role in the proper functioning of the engine's ignition system.</p></p>
