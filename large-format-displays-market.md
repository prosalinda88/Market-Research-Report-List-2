<p><p>Large Format Displays (LFD) are types of displays used for commercial and public information purposes. LED Backlit LFDs utilize light-emitting diodes (LEDs) to illuminate the screen, offering better energy efficiency and color reproduction. They are thinner, lighter, and have a longer lifespan compared to other displays. CCFL LFDs, on the other hand, use cold cathode fluorescent lamps for backlighting. Although CCFL LFDs are less energy-efficient and have shorter lifespans, they are more cost-effective and provide good image quality. Both types are popular choices for advertising, information display, and digital signage applications.</p></p>
