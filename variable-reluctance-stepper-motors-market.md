<p><p>Variable reluctance stepper motors are a type of stepper motor that operate on the principle of varying the reluctance of the magnetic circuit to produce motion. These motors consist of a rotor with soft iron teeth and a stator with multiple windings, typically made of copper wire. When current is applied to the windings, the magnetic field induces magnetic forces that align the rotor teeth with the stator teeth, resulting in precise rotational motion.</p><p>The variable reluctance stepper motors market is expected to witness substantial growth during the forecast period.
