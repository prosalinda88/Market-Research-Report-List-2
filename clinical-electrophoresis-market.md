<p><p>The Clinical Electrophoresis market consists of various types of techniques used in laboratory settings for analyzing and separating different molecules in biological samples. Slab Electrophoresis involves separating molecules on a gel within a horizontal or vertical apparatus. Zone Electrophoresis separates molecules based on their mobility within a medium. Paper Electrophoresis utilizes cellulose acetate or filter paper to separate molecules. Gel Electrophoresis utilizes a gel matrix to separate molecules based on size and charge. Isoelectric-Focusing separates molecules based on their isoelectric point. Immune-Electrophoresis is used to study antigens and antibodies. Capillary Electrophoresis uses narrow capillaries for separating molecules based on charge and size.</p></p>
