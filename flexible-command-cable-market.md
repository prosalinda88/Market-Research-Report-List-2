<p><p>Flexible command cable refers to a type of cable commonly used in various industries for transmitting control signals and power to different devices. It is available in two market types: single core and multi-core. </p><p>Single core cables consist of a single conductor, making them suitable for applications where only one signal or power supply is needed. They are commonly used in smaller electronic devices and for low-power requirements.</p><p>On the other hand, multi-core cables consist of multiple conductors within a single cable sheath. This makes them suitable for applications that require multiple signals or power supplies to be transmitted simultaneously. Multi-core cables are commonly used in larger machinery, automation systems, and industrial environments.</p></p>
