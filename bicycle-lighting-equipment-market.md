<p><ul><li>Halogen Lights</li><li>Xenon Lights</li><li>LED Lights</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bicycle lighting equipment market is categorized into three types: halogen lights, xenon lights, and LED lights. Halogen lights use a halogen gas-filled bulb, emitting a bright yellowish light. Xenon lights deploy xenon gas for illumination, producing a bright white light. LED lights utilize light-emitting diodes, which emit light when an electric current passes through them, resulting in a powerful and energy-efficient illumination. These different types offer cyclists various options for their lighting needs, including brightness, color output, and energy consumption.</p></p>
