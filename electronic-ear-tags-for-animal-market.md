<p><p>Electronic ear tags are used in the animal market for identification and tracking purposes. Half duplex tags transmit data one way, allowing for information such as the animal's identification number to be read by a reader device. Full duplex tags can transmit and receive data, enabling two-way communication between the tag and reader. Both types aid in managing and monitoring animal health, movement, and ownership records. However, full duplex tags offer more advanced capabilities, such as real-time data exchange and remote control functionality.</p></p>
