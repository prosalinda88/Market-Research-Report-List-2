<p><p>Industrial Mobile Racking is a storage system used in warehouses and distribution centers to maximize storage capacity while minimizing the footprint. It is designed to provide high-density storage by eliminating the need for static aisles. This system allows for the movement of racks on wheeled bases, which are mounted on top of rails. It provides better space utilization compared to traditional static racking systems by reducing aisle space, thus increasing storage capacity.</p><p>The Industrial Mobile Racking Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.9% during the forecast period.
