<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Rubber molding press market types include vertical and horizontal models. Vertical presses are designed with a vertical structure, where the mold is positioned vertically. They are ideal for producing small to medium-sized rubber products and provide high accuracy and efficiency. On the other hand, horizontal presses have a horizontal structure and the mold is placed horizontally. These presses are suitable for larger or longer rubber products and offer greater flexibility in terms of mold size and orientation. Both types of presses cater to different manufacturing needs in the rubber industry.</p></p>
