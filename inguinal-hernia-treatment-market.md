<p><p>Inguinal hernia treatment involves surgical procedures to repair a hernia in the groin area, specifically in the inguinal canal. When the intestines or fatty tissues protrude through a weak spot in the abdominal muscles, it leads to the development of an inguinal hernia. The treatment primarily aims to alleviate the symptoms, prevent complications, and repair the hernia.</p><p>The surgical repair of an inguinal hernia can be conducted through two methods: open surgery and laparoscopic surgery. Open surgery involves making an incision near the groin area, pushing the hernia back into place, and reinforcing the weak spot with sutures or mesh. Laparoscopic surgery, on the other hand, involves making small incisions through which a laparoscope and surgical tools are inserted to repair the hernia.</p><p>The global inguinal hernia treatment market is anticipated to witness steady growth during the forecast period.
