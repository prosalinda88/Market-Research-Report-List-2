On the other hand, motorized smoke dampers are specifically designed to restrict the movement of smoke, helping to facilitate safe evacuation during a fire by keeping smoke contained. Both types of dampers play a crucial role in maintaining fire safety and preventing the spread of fire and smoke within buildings.</p></p>
