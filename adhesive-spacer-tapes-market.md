<p><p>Adhesive spacer tapes are used to create gaps or spaces between objects, providing isolation or cushioning. They come in two main types: double-sided and single-sided. Double-sided adhesive spacer tapes have adhesive on both sides, allowing them to stick to two surfaces simultaneously, making them suitable for joining materials together securely. On the other hand, single-sided spacer tapes have adhesive only on one side, making them ideal for adhering objects to surfaces while maintaining a gap or distance. Both types play a crucial role in various industries where bonding, insulation, or protection is required.</p></p>
