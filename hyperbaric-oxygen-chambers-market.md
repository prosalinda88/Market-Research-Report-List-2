<p><p>The Hyperbaric Oxygen Chambers market is categorized into two main types: Monoplace Chambers and Multiplace Chambers. Monoplace Chambers are designed for one patient at a time and are generally smaller, more affordable, and easier to operate. On the other hand, Multiplace Chambers can accommodate multiple patients simultaneously, making them more suitable for hospitals or large medical facilities. Multiplace Chambers also require specialized medical personnel to operate them properly. Both types of chambers provide pressurized oxygen therapy for various medical conditions, but their capacity and usage differ.</p></p>
