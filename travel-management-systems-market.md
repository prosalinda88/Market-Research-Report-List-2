<p><p>A travel management system (TMS) is a software solution that helps businesses manage their travel bookings, expenses, and itineraries. TMS can be categorized into two types: On-premise and Cloud-based. On-premise TMS refers to a system that is installed and maintained on the company's own servers and infrastructure. It offers more control and customization but requires higher upfront costs and maintenance efforts. Cloud-based TMS, on the other hand, operates on remote servers managed by a third-party provider, offering ease of access, scalability, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
