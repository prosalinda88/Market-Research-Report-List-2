<p><strong>The X-Ray Grating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorption Grating</li><li>Phase Grating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-ray grating market consists of different types of gratings, including absorption grating and phase grating. Absorption grating works by absorbing X-ray photons and measuring the transmitted intensity. It provides excellent efficiency and higher spectral resolution. On the other hand, phase grating operates by separating X-rays based on their phase shift. It offers superior coherence and higher diffraction efficiency. Both types of gratings are widely used in X-ray imaging and spectroscopy applications, serving different purposes and meeting specific requirements in the market.</p></p>
