<p><ul><li>Fluorescent Lamp</li><li>LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The softbox lighting kit market offers two main types: fluorescent lamp and LED. Fluorescent lamp softboxes utilize gas-discharge tubes to produce light. These softboxes are often more affordable but have a shorter lifespan and consume more energy. On the other hand, LED softboxes use light-emitting diodes, making them more efficient, long-lasting, and energy-saving. Although LED softboxes tend to be more expensive upfront, they offer better color accuracy and versatility. Ultimately, the choice between fluorescent lamp or LED softboxes depends on factors such as budget, desired lifespan, energy efficiency, and lighting needs.</p></p>
