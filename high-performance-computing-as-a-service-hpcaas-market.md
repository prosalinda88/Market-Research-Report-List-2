<p><p>High-Performance Computing as a Service (HPCaaS) market includes three types: Colocation, Hosted Private Cloud, and Public Cloud. In the Colocation market, companies rent a physical HPC infrastructure from a third-party provider and manage their software on-site. Hosted Private Cloud involves dedicated HPC resources hosted by a service provider at their data center. Public Cloud market provides shared HPC services over the internet, with resources allocated on-demand and managed by the cloud service provider. These market types offer businesses varying levels of control, cost-effectiveness, and scalability options for their high-performance computing needs.</p></p>
