<p><p>Veterinary dental scalers are instruments used for cleaning and removing tartar from animals' teeth. There are two types of dental scalers: ultrasonic and mechanical. Ultrasonic scalers use high-frequency vibrations to break down the tartar, while mechanical scalers require manual action to remove the tartar. Ultrasonic scalers are commonly used due to their efficiency and effectiveness in tartar removal. On the other hand, mechanical scalers are less common and may be preferred for specific dental procedures or in situations where ultrasonic scalers are not suitable.</p></p>
