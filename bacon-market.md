<p><p>There are different market types for bacon depending on the curing process used. Dry cured bacon is made by rubbing the meat with a mixture of salt, sugar, and spices and then letting it air-dry for a period of time. Immersion cured bacon involves soaking the meat in a brine solution that contains salt, sugar, and other flavorings. Besides these two main types, there are also other variations of bacon available in the market, such as maple bacon, applewood-smoked bacon, peppered bacon, and more, which offer unique flavors and tastes to cater to consumers' preferences.</p></p>
